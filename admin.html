📚 Publishing Guide

Step 1: Write Your Post

Fill in all the fields in the editor tab. Your draft is automatically saved to your browser.

Step 2: Preview Your Post

Click the "👁️ Preview" button to see how your post will look on your blog.

Step 3: Publish

Click "✨ Publish Post" to generate the HTML code.

Step 4: Add to Your Repository

Create post file:
  • Go to your GitHub repo
  • Click "Add file" → "Create new file"
  • Name: posts/your-post-name.html
  • Paste the post HTML
  • Commit with message: "Add blog post: Your Title"
Update blog.html:
  • Edit blog.html
  • Find </div class="posts-list">
  • Add the blog card HTML before it
  • Commit the changes

⚡ Pro Tips

  • Keep post titles under 100 characters
  • Write excerpts that hook readers
  • Use categories consistently
  • Estimate reading time accurately
  • Test your post on mobile devices
